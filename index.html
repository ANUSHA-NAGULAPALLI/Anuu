About Srisailam

Srisailam is a town in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh, known for the famous Srisailam Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

The town is situated on the banks of the Krishna River and is surrounded by beautiful hills and forests.

Attractions

  • Srisailam Temple
  • Nagarjunasagar Dam
  • Wildlife Sanctuary
